mission `Looting a Jump Drive (The First Time)`
  description `There is a disabled Quarg ship in the Southern Wormhole 2 system. Loot the jump drive and return for payment. This will require 20 cargo space. You may want to use a cloaking device so you don't die.`
  landing
  job
  waypoint "Southern Wormhole 2"

  to offer
    has "Choose the Coalition: done"

  npc
        personality derelict
            confusion 0
        government "Quarg"
        system "Southern Wormhole 2"
        ship "Quarg Wardragon (jump only)" "Quarg Wardragon"

  on complete
    dialog `A grateful Heliarch official gives you 5 million credits for the Jump Drive you looted. You may now buy looted Quarg outfits in the future when you come to the Ring of Fellowship. You must leave and land again before they will become available.`
    outfit "Jump Drive" -1
    payment 5000000
    event `quarg jump drives looted`
    event `quarg ships looted`
