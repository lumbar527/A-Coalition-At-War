    dialog "You tell [Contact] that the anomaly is a wormhole and there are battles going on between the Heliarch ships and the Quarg."

    event "bad news"

If you don't want to continue the story yet, leave and don't go to either world until you are ready."

  description `Observe the Heliarch's New Ship Prototype`
  job

  waypoint "Southern Wormhole 2"

  on accept
    dialog `Observe the Heliarch's new ship prototype in Southern Wormhole 2. Return to Forpelog with your observations. Do not engage any ships there, simply watch from a safe distance.`
    dialog `The Quarg are sending one of their new "Skydragon" ships with you on this mission.`

  npc
        personality staying
            confusion 0
        government "Heliarch"
        system "Southern Wormhole 2"
        ship "Heliarch Longsword" "Heliarch Longsword"

  npc
        personality heroic disables
            confusion 0
        government "Quarg"
     ship "Quarg Skydragon" "Quarg Skydragon"

  on complete

    dialog `Since you have proven yourself trustworthy, we do not want you to be unable to survive future missions. We are therefore allowing you to purchase our normal ships.
